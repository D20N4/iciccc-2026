ICICCC-2026

Submission Guidelines

Author Instructions

To ensure a smooth peer-review process and high-quality publication in IEEE Xplore, all authors must strictly adhere to the following guidelines.

01

General Requirements

  • Originality: Submissions must showcase unpublished, original work that is not currently under review by any other conference or journal.
  • Language: All manuscripts must be written in clear, concise English.
  • Plagiarism: All papers will be screened using anti-plagiarism software. ICICCC-2026 maintains a zero-tolerance policy toward plagiarism in accordance with IEEE standards.
02

Manuscript Formatting

  • Template: Authors must use the standard IEEE Two-Column Conference Template.
  • Page Limit: The final manuscript must not exceed 6 pages, including all figures, tables, and references.
  • File Format: Submissions must be uploaded in PDF format only.
03

Peer Review Process

  • Review Type: The conference follows a Double-Blind (Anonymous) review process.
  • Anonymization: Authors must remove names, affiliations, and any self-identifying references from the initial submission file.
  • Evaluation: Evaluated by at least 3 expert reviewers based on originality, technical quality, and relevance.
04

Final Submission (Camera-Ready)

  • Notification of Acceptance: July 30, 2026.
  • Deadline: Accepted papers must be revised and uploaded by August 18, 2026.
  • Copyright: Authors of accepted papers must transfer copyright to IEEE to facilitate publication.
05

Presentation and Registration

  • Mandatory Registration: At least one author must complete registration and pay the fee to be included in the proceedings.
  • Presentation: Accepted papers must be presented during the conference (in-person or virtually).
  • IEEE Xplore: Papers that are not presented will be removed from the final submission to IEEE Xplore.
