Important Notes:

Offline Mode:

All presenters and participants are required to be physically present at the venue for their sessions.

Mandatory Attendance:

Accepted papers must be presented by one of the authors to be uploaded to IEEE Xplore.

Accommodation:

Subsidized guest house/hostel available upon request (not included in registration fee).
